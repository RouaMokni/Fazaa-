Why Aerospace ?

Aerospace The aerospace industry is also exploring the use of drones to provide inspections of commercial aircraft. With traditional technology, visual inspections can take up to six hours, but drones could significantly reduce the amount of time and provide increased accuracy and ease of documentation.

Drones..

Drones are unmanned aircraft whose pilot is automatic or remotely controlled, for civilian use or for the benefit of the armed or security forces — police, customs — of a State. Depending on the capacities sought, their mass varies from a few grams to several tons. Their autonomy can reach up to several tens of hours (compared to the typical two hours of autonomy of a fighter).
